INTEGIRLS Phoenix

Our Mission

INTEGIRLS is a nonprofit teen-led organization with a mission to bridge the gender gap in STEM through exciting math opportunities! All our programs are offered free of cost to our participants.

Upcoming Events

For the 2025–2026 school year, INTEGIRLS chapter will be hosting two competitions — one in the fall and one in the spring. Our events are open to all female and non-binary-identifying middle and high schoolers. Both events are free of charge for everyone. No prior experience is required! Sign-ups for the fall event will open once the date is finalized!

FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

I’m interested but have no math competition experience. Can I still attend?


Of course! Many participants have started their math competition journey with INTEGIRLS.

I do not have an INTEGIRLS chapter near my area, but I am interested in participating. Can I still attend?


Absolutely! We welcome students from Phoenix and other regions. Reach out anytime!

Who can I contact if I have any questions?


You can contact us through Email or Instagram at the links below.

I do not identify as female, but I would like to participate. How can I do that?


If you do not identify as female or non-binary, please reach out for other opportunities!

How can I contribute?


You can volunteer or donate here.

How many competitions will you conduct throughout the year?


Two competitions per year—one in fall and one in spring.
