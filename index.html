INTEGIRLS Phoenix

Our Mission

INTEGIRLS is a nonprofit teen-led organization with a mission to bridge the gender gap in STEM through exciting math opportunities! All our programs are offered free of cost to our participants.

Upcoming Events

For the 2025–2026 school year, INTEGIRLS Phoenix will be hosting two competitions — one in the fall and one in the spring. Our events are open to all female and non-binary-identifying middle and high schoolers. Both events are free of charge for everyone. No prior experience is required! Sign-ups for the fall event will open once the date is finalized!

FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

I’m interested but have no math competition experience. Can I still attend?


Of course! Many participants have started their math competition journey with INTEGIRLS. If you’d like tips on how to prepare, email us anytime. We’d love to help — and hope to see you at our event!

I do not have an INTEGIRLS chapter near my area, but I am interested in participating. Can I still attend?


Absolutely! We welcome students from Phoenix and other regions. You may reach out using the links at the bottom of this page.

Who can I contact if I have any questions?


You can contact us through Email or Instagram at the links below. It may take up to two weeks for an email reply — if urgent, please message us on Instagram or include “URGENT” in your email subject line.

I do not identify as female, but I would like to participate. How can I do that?


If you do not identify as female or non-binary, we encourage you to explore other opportunities we share. You can contact phoenix@integirls.org for more information.

How can I contribute?


You can volunteer by emailing us at phoenix@integirls.org or donate to our nonprofit here. Every bit of help makes a difference!

How many competitions will you conduct throughout the year?


We plan to host two competitions per year: one in the fall and one in the spring. More details will be available soon on our website.
